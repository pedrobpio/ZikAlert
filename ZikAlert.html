ZikAlert was created in an attempt to inform and teach people about the zika virus.

With the app one can learn about causes, prevention, symptoms, and treatment. One can get the latest news from different RSS feeds. One can also use a checklist to be reminded of the best practices to follow.

The app contains all the information you need to be on the safe side!
